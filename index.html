About Hushh

Hushh is a product-based company building the infrastructure for user-controlled data. Their platform enables individuals to aggregate, manage, and monetize their personal data while maintaining full ownership and consent control. Hushh products include a Wallet App for personal data vaults, a Browser Companion for browsing privacy, and APIs that let businesses access consented user data — creating a transparent, trust-first data economy.
